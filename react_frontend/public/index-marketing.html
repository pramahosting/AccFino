Why AccFino

What current platforms
don't give you

The tools Australian businesses actually need are locked behind premium tiers, expensive add-ons, or simply don't exist on most platforms. AccFino includes them from Starter.

01
ML forecasting on every plan
Most platforms treat cash flow forecasting as a premium feature — if they offer it at all. AccFino's ensemble ML model runs on your actual transaction history and predicts next-month cash flow with confidence intervals. Included from Starter, not locked behind Enterprise.
02
Crypto CGT — built in, not bolted on
Current platforms have no crypto CGT capability. Australian investors managing crypto alongside business accounts pay for separate tools, manually reconcile, and risk ATO audit exposure. AccFino handles it natively — import, normalise, calculate, lodge.
03
AI classification that learns your rules
Generic ML classifiers misfire on industry-specific transactions. AccFino's RDR rule engine lets you define deterministic rules that override the LLM — "any payment to this ABN is always Office Supplies." Accuracy compounds over time. Other platforms start over each session.
04
AU data — always, not optionally
Many current accounting platforms store Australian business data offshore by default. AccFino is designed and hosted in Australia from the ground up — not as a compliance checkbox. Your transaction data, invoices, and customer records never leave AU infrastructure.
